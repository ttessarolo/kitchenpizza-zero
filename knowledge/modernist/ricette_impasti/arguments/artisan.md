# Artisan Pizza Dough Recipes

<!-- p.54-59 -->

## 1. Artisan Pizza Dough (Master Recipe) <!-- p.54 -->

**Type:** Master Recipe | **Time:** Active 15-20 min / Inactive 48½ h | **Yield:** ~1.1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| **Poolish** | | |
| High-gluten bread flour, 13%-14% protein | 75 g | 100 |
| Water | 75 g | 100 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.08 g | 0.11 |
| **Dough** | | |
| Water, 21°C | 375 g | 68.18 |
| Instant dry yeast | 2.6 g | 0.47 |
| High-gluten bread flour, 13%-14% protein | 550 g | 100 |
| Diastatic malt powder | 13 g | 2.36 |
| Fine salt | 12.5 g | 2.27 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 20 g | 3.64 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 625 g (100%), Water 450 g (72%), Fat 20 g (3.2%), Diastatic malt powder 13 g (2.08%), Salt 12.5 g (2%), Yeast 2.68 g (0.43%)

**Flour:** Giusto's High Performer High Protein Unbleached Flour.

**Process:** Poolish 12-16 h -> Mix to full gluten development -> Bench rest 20 min -> Divide 360 g -> Ball -> Cold-proof 1-2 d at 4°C

Easy-to-work-with dough, excellent choice for home cooks. Can reball after 2 d for up to 5 d total.

## 2. Modernist Artisan Pizza Dough <!-- p.56 -->

Same as master plus:
- High-methoxyl pectin: 12.5 g (1 Tbsp + 1 tsp), 2.2%
- Dough relaxer options: Bromelain (0.0005%), Papain (0.01%), Meat tenderizer (0.01%) - all added to flour

Adds Pectin 12.5 g (2%) to net contents.

|---|---|---|
