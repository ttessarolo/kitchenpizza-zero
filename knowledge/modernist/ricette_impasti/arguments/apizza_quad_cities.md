# Apizza & Quad Cities Pizza Dough Recipes

## 1. Apizza Dough <!-- p.51 -->

**Type:** Submaster Recipe | **Time:** Active 10-15 min / Inactive 4 h | **Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| **Poolish** | | |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein | 55 g | 100 |
| Water | 55 g | 100 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.06 g | 0.11 |
| **Dough** | | |
| Water, 21°C | 330 g | 60 |
| Instant dry yeast | 2 g | 0.36 |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein | 550 g | 100 |
| Fine salt | 11 g | 2 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 605 g (100%), Water 385 g (63.63%), Salt 11 g (1.82%), Yeast 2.06 g (0.33%)

**Divide:** 500 g. **Proof:** 3-3½ h at 21°C or 2-2½ h at 27°C/65% RH.

## 3. Quad Cities Pizza Dough <!-- p.52 -->

**Type:** Submaster Recipe | **Time:** Active 15 min / Inactive 48½ h | **Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| **Poolish** | | |
| Flour, 11.5%-14% protein | 60 g | 100 |
| Water | 60 g | 100 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.06 g | 0.1 |
| **Dough** | | |
| Water, 21°C | 260 g | 49.06 |
| Instant dry yeast | 2.8 g | 0.53 |
| Flour, 11.5%-14% protein | 530 g | 100 |
| Malted barley syrup | 60 g | 11.32 |
| Fine salt | 14.5 g | 2.74 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 20 g | 3.77 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 590 g (100%), Water 332.6 g (56.37%), Sugar 42.8 g (7.25%), Fat 20 g (3.39%), Salt 14.5 g (2.46%), Yeast 2.86 g (0.48%)

**Key:** High sugar content from malted barley syrup. Cold-proof 1-2 d at 4°C.

**Flour:** General Mills All Trumps, Tony Gemignani CA Artisan Type 00, Bouncer Premium High Gluten, Pillsbury Balancer Hi Gluten, or Grain Craft Power High Gluten.

## 4. Quad Cities Pizza Dough Variation <!-- p.53 -->

**Time:** Active 10 min / Inactive 4 h | **Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C | 375 g | 61.47 |
| Malted barley syrup | 30 g | 4.9 |
| Instant dry yeast | 2.2 g | 0.36 |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein | 610 g | 100 |
| Fine salt | 12.2 g | 2 |

No poolish. Autolyse 30 min. Bench rest 1 h. Proof 3 h at 21°C or 2 h 40 min at 27°C/65% RH; optional cold-proof 2 d at 4°C.

Closer to what pizza makers in Quad Cities shared.
