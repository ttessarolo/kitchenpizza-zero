# Thin-Crust Pizza Dough Recipes

<!-- p.26-33 -->

## 1. Thin-Crust Pizza Dough (Master Recipe) <!-- p.26 -->

**Type:** Master Recipe | **Time:** Active 15 min / Inactive 3½ h | **Yield:** ~1.1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| **Poolish** | | |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein | 55 g | 100 |
| Water | 55 g | 100 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.06 g | 0.1 |
| **Dough** | | |
| Water, 21°C / 70°F | 370 g | 68.52 |
| Instant dry yeast | 4.8 g | 0.89 |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein | 540 g | 100 |
| Fine-ground cornmeal | 65 g | 12.04 |
| Fine salt | 13 g | 2.41 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 595 g (100%), Water 425 g (71.43%), Cornmeal 65 g (10.92%), Salt 13 g (2.18%), Yeast 4.86 g (0.82%)

**Process:** Poolish 12-16 h at 21°C -> Mix to full gluten development -> Bench rest 20 min -> Divide (370 g for 50 cm, 275 g for 40 cm) -> Ball preshape -> Proof 2-3 h at 21°C, optional cold-proof 1 d at 4°C

**Flour:** Ceresota/Heckers Unbleached All-Purpose Flour recommended.

**Sizes:** 50 cm / 20 in (370 g) or 40 cm / 16 in (275 g)

## 2. Modernist Thin-Crust Pizza Dough <!-- p.28 -->

**Type:** Ingredient Variation | Same base as master plus:
- Polydextrose: 6 g (1.1%) - add with flour
- Cornmeal reduced to 55 g (10.19%)
- Dough relaxer options available

**Net Contents:** Flour 595 g (100%), Water 425 g (71.43%), Cornmeal 55 g (9.24%), Salt 13 g (2.18%), Polydextrose 6 g (1%), Yeast 4.86 g (0.82%)

| Ingredient | % | Add to |
|---|---|---|
| Pineapple juice, fresh | 0.01 | water |
| Bromelain, powdered | 0.0005 | flour |
| Kiwi juice, fresh | 0.08 | water |
| Papain, powdered | 0.01 | flour |
| Papaya juice, fresh | 0.03 | water |
| Adolph's meat tenderizer | 0.01 | flour |
