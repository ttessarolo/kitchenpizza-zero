# Pizza Gourmet Dough

<!-- p.73-74 -->

## Pizza Gourmet Dough (Submaster Recipe)

**Type:** Submaster Recipe | **Time:** Active 10-15 min / Inactive 4¼ h | **Yield:** ~1 kg
**Shape:** Four 20 cm / 8 in pizzas

### Ingredients

| Ingredient | Weight | Volume | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|---|
| Hong Kong flour or pastry flour | 600 g | 5 cups | 100 |
| Water, 21°C / 70°F | 340 g | 1½ cups | 56.67 |
| Sugar | 64 g | 5 Tbsp + 2 tsp | 10.67 |
| Fine salt | 12 g | 2⅛ tsp | 1.88 |
| Baking powder | 6.6 g | 1½ tsp | 1.1 |
| Instant dry yeast | 6.5 g | 2¼ tsp + ⅛ tsp | 1.08 |

### Net Contents

| Component | Weight | % |
|---|---|---|
| Flour | 600 g | 100 |
| Water | 340 g | 56.67 |
| Sugar | 64 g | 10.67 |
| Salt | 12 g | 1.88 |
| Baking powder | 6.6 g | 1.1 |
| Yeast | 6.5 g | 1.08 |

### Key Characteristics

- **Unique flour:** Hong Kong flour (commonly used for bao) or pastry flour
- **Dual leavening:** Both yeast AND baking powder
- **High sugar:** 10.67% - significantly higher than any other pizza dough
- **Mixing method:** Paddle attachment on medium speed to full gluten development (unusual for a soft flour)
- **Cooking method:** Steamed before baking (see page 255)

### Process

**MIX:** Combine flour, water, sugar, salt, baking powder, and yeast. Mix with paddle on medium speed to full gluten development. Windowpane test. Transfer to lightly floured worktable.

**BULK FERMENT:** 45 min at 21°C / 70°F; cover well.

**DIVIDE:** 250 g.

**PRESHAPE:** Ball (see page 20); flatten to a 2.5 cm / 1 in thick disc; cover well.

**PROOF:** 3½ h at 21°C / 70°F, covered, or 2½ h at 27°C / 80°F and 65% RH.

**Assembly, steaming, and baking:** see page 255.

### Notes

Hong Kong flour is commonly used for making bao, which share some characteristics with pizza gourmet dough. For more on where to purchase it, see Resources, page 3:377.
