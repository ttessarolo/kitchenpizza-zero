# Deep-Dish Pizza Dough Recipes

<!-- p.34-37 -->

## 1. Deep-Dish Pizza Dough (Master Recipe) <!-- p.34 -->

**Type:** Master Recipe | **Time:** Active 15-20 min / Inactive 2 h | **Yield:** ~700 g

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C / 70°F | 225 g | 59.21 |
| Instant dry yeast | 3.1 g | 0.82 |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein | 380 g | 100 |
| Fine-ground cornmeal | 40 g | 10.53 |
| Lard, softened | 18 g | 4.74 |
| Butter, softened | 18 g | 4.74 |
| Fine salt | 8 g | 2.11 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 380 g (100%), Water 228 g (60%), Cornmeal 40 g (10.53%), Fat 33 g (8.68%), Salt 8 g (2.11%), Yeast 3.1 g (0.82%)

**Process:** Mix -> Bench rest 20 min -> Divide (do not divide for 32 cm; 230 g for 21 cm) -> Proof 1½ h at 21°C, optional cold-proof 1 d at 4°C

**Flour:** Ceresota/Heckers Unbleached All-Purpose Flour. Can double butter if omitting lard.

## 2. Modernist Deep-Dish Pizza Dough <!-- p.36 -->

Same as master plus:
- Freeze-dried cheddar cheese powder: 40 g (10.53%)
- Freeze-dried butter powder: 40 g (10.53%)
- Polydextrose: 3.8 g (1%)

Add cheese/butter powders and polydextrose with flour. Powders aid browning and flavor.
