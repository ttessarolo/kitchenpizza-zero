# Gluten-Free Pizza Dough Recipes

<!-- p.100-107 -->

All GF recipes use paddle attachment (not hook). Each has a Caputo Fiore Glut variation.

## 1. Gluten-Free Thin-Crust Pizza Dough <!-- p.100 -->

**Time:** Active 15 min / Inactive 3½ h | **Yield:** ~1 kg
**Shape:** Three 50 cm / 20 in or four 40 cm / 16 in pizzas

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C | 412 g | 69.24 |
| Instant dry yeast | 4.8 g | 0.81 |
| Gluten-Free Flour Blend | 595 g | 100 |
| Fine-ground cornmeal | 55 g | 9.24 |
| Fine salt | 13 g | 2.18 |

**Divide:** 360 g (50 cm) or 270 g (40 cm). **Proof:** 2-3 h at 21°C; optional cold-proof 1 d at 4°C.

**Variation:** Replace GF flour blend with Caputo Fiore Glut.

## 2. Gluten-Free Brazilian Thin-Crust Pizza Dough <!-- p.101 -->

**Time:** Active 20-25 min / Inactive 4 h | **Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C | 325 g | 54.17 |
| Instant dry yeast | 3 g | 0.5 |
| Gluten-Free Flour Blend | 600 g | 100 |

## 5. Gluten-Free New York/Artisan Pizza Dough <!-- p.104 -->

**Time:** Active 15-20 min / Inactive 2½ h | **Yield:** ~1.3 kg
**Shape:** Three 35 cm / 14 in pizzas

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C | 460 g | 59.74 |
| Instant dry yeast | 3.5 g | 0.45 |
| Gluten-Free Flour Blend | 770 g | 100 |
| Diastatic malt powder | 20 g | 2.6 |
| Fine salt | 18 g | 2.33 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 25 g | 3.25 |

**Divide:** 430 g. Dust with GF flour, extend with rolling pin to ~35 cm circles. Place in pans oiled with 70 g (⅓ cup) olive oil each.

**Proof:** 2 h at 21°C or 1½ h at 27°C/65% RH.

**Topping:** 160 g (⅓ cup) NY/Artisan Pizza Tomato Sauce and 195 g (2 cups + 2 Tbsp) pizza cheese.

## 6. Gluten-Free Pan Pizza Dough <!-- p.105 -->

**Time:** Active 10-15 min / Inactive 2 h | **Yield:** ~2 kg
**Shape:** One 46 cm by 33 cm / 18 in by 13 in pizza

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Brown rice flour | 570 g | 70.81 |
| Tapioca flour | 200 g | 24.84 |
| Potato starch | 35 g | 4.35 |
| Nonfat milk powder | 25 g | 3.11 |
| Fine salt | 15 g | 1.86 |
| Water, 21°C | 1 kg | 124.22 |
| Sugar | 35 g | 4.35 |
| Instant dry yeast | 30 g | 3.73 |
| Egg white | 50 g | 6.21 |
| Psyllium husk, optional | 50 g | 6.21 |
| Parmesan cheese, grated | 15 g | 1.86 |
| Fresh rosemary, minced | 5 g | 0.62 |

**Key:** Very high hydration (124.22%). Uses egg white and psyllium husk for structure. Parmesan and rosemary optional (for focaccia version).

**Process:** Combine dry ingredients (add Parmesan/rosemary for focaccia). Dissolve sugar and yeast in water; let stand 5 min. Whisk egg white with psyllium; let stand 1 min. Add egg white to yeast mixture, add dry ingredients, mix with paddle 3 min, scrape, mix 3 min more. Transfer to oiled, parchment-lined half sheet pan. Spread with spatula. Proof 2 h at 21°C or 1½ h at 27°C/65% RH.

Works for NY Square, focaccia, or al taglio. For al taglio, multiply by 0.75 for 1.5 kg dough.

## 8. Gluten-Free Flour Blend <!-- p.107 -->

**Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Volume | % |
|---|---|---|---|
| White rice flour | 225 g | 1⅓ cups | 23.2 |
| Brown rice flour | 100 g | ¾ cup | 10.31 |
| Glutinous rice flour | 70 g | ⅔ cup | 7.22 |
| Cornstarch | 350 g | 2¾ cups | 36.08 |
| Tapioca starch | 225 g | 1¾ cups | 23.2 |
| Nonfat milk powder* | 100 g | ¾ cup + 2 Tbsp | 10.31 |
| Xanthan gum | 10 g | 1 Tbsp | 1.03 |
| Transglutaminase T1 or WM (optional) | 9.7 g | 1 Tbsp | 1 |

*Omit milk powder for vegan blend.*

**Method:** Whisk all ingredients together. Process in batches in food processor until finely ground (1-2 min per batch). Sift each batch.

**Storage:** Without transglutaminase: 2 mo at room temperature. With transglutaminase: MUST freeze (enzymes don't survive at room temp). Freeze up to 3 mo in single-recipe portions. Warm at room temperature 1-2 h before use.

**Transglutaminase note:** Acts as "meat glue" binding proteins. Produces chew and consistency closer to wheat bread. Does not increase volume.
