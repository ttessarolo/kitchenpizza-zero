# Focaccia Dough Recipes

<!-- p.60-63 -->

## 1. Focaccia Dough (Master Recipe) <!-- p.60 -->

**Type:** Master Recipe | **Time:** Active 20-25 min / Inactive 6 h | **Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| **Poolish** | | |
| High-gluten bread flour, 13.5%-15% protein | 65 g | 100 |
| Water | 65 g | 100 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.07 g | 0.11 |
| **Dough** | | |
| Water, 21°C | 335 g | 83.75 |
| Liquid levain, mature | 60 g | 15 |
| Malted barley syrup | 5 g | 1.25 |
| Instant dry yeast | 1.2 g | 0.3 |
| High-gluten bread flour, 13.5%-15% protein | 400 g | 100 |
| Fine salt | 10 g | 2.5 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 20 g | 5 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 495 g (100%), Water 432 g (87.27%), Fat 20 g (4.04%), Salt 10 g (2.02%), Sugar 3.5 g (0.71%), Yeast 1.27 g (0.25%)

**Flour:** General Mills All Trumps Bakers High Gluten Flour.

**Process:** Poolish 12-16 h -> Mix with double hydration: combine 285 g water with levain, syrup, yeast, poolish; add flour; autolyse 30 min; stir salt into remaining water; add salt mixture; mix on high to medium gluten development; add oil -> Lightly oiled tub, rectangle shape, four-edge fold.

**Bulk Ferment:** 2½ h at 21°C with 4 four-edge folds (every 30 min after first 30 min) to full gluten development. Rest 30 min after final fold.

**Preshape:** Transfer to oiled half sheet pan, stretch to fill pan.

**Proof:** 3 h at 21°C or 2 h at 27°C/65% RH. Stretch halfway through.

**Key:** Uses both poolish AND levain. Very high hydration (87.27%). Mix to medium (not full) gluten development; achieve full development through folding.

## 2. Modernist Focaccia Dough <!-- p.63 -->

Same as master plus:
- High-methoxyl pectin: 3.2 g (1 tsp), 0.8%
- Shortening, lard, or ghee replaces olive oil
- Dough relaxer options: Bromelain (0.0005%) best in tests
