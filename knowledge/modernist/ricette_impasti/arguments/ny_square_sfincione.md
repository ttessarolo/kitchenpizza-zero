# NY Square Pizza & Sfincione Dough Recipes

<!-- p.64-67 -->

## 1. New York Square Pizza Dough (Master Recipe) <!-- p.64 -->

**Type:** Master Recipe | **Time:** Active 20-25 min / Inactive 6 h | **Yield:** ~700 g

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| **Poolish** | | |
| High-gluten bread flour, 13.5%-15% protein | 40 g | 100 |
| Water | 40 g | 100 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.05 g | 0.13 |
| **Dough** | | |
| Water, 21°C | 255 g | 71.83 |
| Instant dry yeast | 1.95 g | 0.54 |
| High-gluten bread flour | 355 g | 100 |
| Fine salt | 7.9 g | 2.23 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 15 g | 4.23 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 395 g (100%), Water 295 g (74.68%), Fat 15 g (3.8%), Salt 7.9 g (2%), Yeast 1.95 g (0.49%)

**Shape:** One 46 cm by 33 cm / 18 in by 13 in pizza (half sheet pan).

**Process:** Poolish 12-16 h -> Mix with autolyse 30 min; salt added after; oil added in slow stream; mix to medium gluten development -> Oiled tub, rectangle, four-edge fold -> Bulk ferment 2½ h with 4 folds -> Oil half sheet pan -> Preshape in pan -> Proof 3 h at 21°C or 2 h at 27°C/65% RH.

## 2. Modernist New York Square Pizza Dough <!-- p.66 -->

Same as master plus:
- High-methoxyl pectin: 8 g (2¾ tsp + ⅛ tsp), 2.25%
- Shortening, lard, or clarified butter replaces olive oil
- Dough relaxer options

### Dough Relaxer Options

| Ingredient | % | Add to |
|---|---|---|
| Papaya juice, fresh | 0.03 | water |
| Adolph's meat tenderizer | 0.01 | flour |
| Bromelain, powdered | 0.0005 | flour |
| Kiwi juice, fresh | 0.08 | water |
| Papain, powdered | 0.01 | flour |

Papaya juice and meat tenderizer performed best.

## 4. Sfincione Dough (aka Sfinciuni Dough) <!-- p.67 -->

**Type:** Submaster Recipe | **Time:** Active 10-15 min / Inactive 3¾ h | **Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C | 390 g | 68.42 |
| Instant dry yeast | 5 g | 0.88 |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein | 570 g | 100 |
| Fine salt | 11 g | 1.93 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 30 g | 5.26 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 570 g (100%), Water 390 g (68.42%), Fat 30 g (5.26%), Salt 11 g (1.93%), Yeast 5 g (0.88%)

**Shape:** One 46 cm by 33 cm / 18 in by 13 in pizza.

**Process:** Mix to full gluten development with oil added last in slow stream -> Oiled tub, rectangle, four-edge fold -> Bench rest 20 min -> Preshape in oiled pan -> Proof 2-3 h at 21°C or 1-2 h at 27°C/65% RH.

**Flour:** Central Milling Organic Artisan Bakers Craft Bread Flour.
