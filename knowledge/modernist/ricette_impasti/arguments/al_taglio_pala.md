# Al Taglio & Roman Pizza alla Pala Dough Recipes

<!-- p.68-73 -->

## 1. High-Hydration al Taglio Pizza Dough (Master Recipe) <!-- p.68 -->

**Type:** Master Recipe | **Time:** Active 15-20 min / Inactive 5½ h | **Yield:** ~1.4 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C | 480 g | 76.19 |
| Instant dry yeast | 3.7 g | 0.59 |
| Liquid levain, mature | 220 g | 34.92 |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein, or 00 flour | 630 g | 100 |
| Diastatic malt powder | 1.5 g | 0.24 |
| Fine salt | 18.5 g | 2.94 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 30 g | 4.76 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 740 g (100%), Water 590 g (79.73%), Fat 30 g (4.02%), Salt 18.5 g (2.5%), Yeast 3.7 g (0.5%), Diastatic malt powder 1.5 g (0.2%)

**Flour:** Polselli Super 00 Flour.

**Key technique:** Double hydration method. Mix first 380 g water with yeast, levain, flour, malt to low gluten development -> Autolyse 30 min -> Stir salt into remaining water -> Mix to medium gluten development -> Add salt mixture -> Add oil in slow stream -> Mix on high to medium gluten development.

**Bulk Ferment:** 2½ h at 21°C with 4 four-edge folds to full gluten development. Rest 30 min.

**Sizes:** 60 cm by 40 cm / 24 in by 16 in (do not divide) or 60 cm by 20 cm / 24 in by 8 in (700 g each).

**Proof:** 3 h at 21°C or 2 h at 27°C/65% RH. Stretch after first hour.

**Dough temp off mixer:** 18-24°C / 64-75°F.

## 3. High-Hydration al Taglio Pizza Dough with Poolish <!-- p.71 -->

Replaces levain with poolish:
- Bread/00 flour: 110 g (100%)
- Water: 110 g (100%)
- Instant dry yeast: 0.11 g (0.1%)

Poolish ferments 12-16 h at 21°C. Then follow master recipe, replacing levain with poolish.

**Net Contents:** Flour 740 g (100%), Water 590 g (79.73%), Fat 30 g (4.05%), Salt 18.5 g (2.5%), Yeast 3.81 g (0.51%), Diastatic malt 1.5 g (0.2%)

## 6. Roman Pizza alla Pala Dough <!-- p.73 -->

**Type:** Submaster Recipe | **Time:** Active 15-20 min / Inactive 5½ h | **Yield:** ~1.4 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water | 470 g | 73.34 |
| Instant dry yeast | 3.8 g | 0.59 |
| Liquid levain, mature | 250 g | 39.06 |
| Bread/00 flour | 640 g | 100 |
| Diastatic malt powder | 1.6 g | 0.25 |
| Fine salt | 19 g | 2.97 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 15 g | 2.34 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 765 g (100%), Water 595 g (77.78%), Salt 19 g (2.48%), Fat 15 g (1.96%), Yeast 3.8 g (0.5%), Diastatic malt 1.6 g (0.2%)

**Flour:** Polselli Super 00, Caputo Pizza a Metro 00, Molino Grassi Slow H24 00, or Le 5 Stagioni Manitoba 00.

**Process:** Same double hydration as al Taglio (first 380 g water) but bulk ferment 2 h with 3 four-edge folds.

**Preshape:** Tight oblong batard (not rectangle). Oiled tub.

**Proof:** 3 h at 21°C or 2 h at 27°C/65% RH; then cold-proof 16 h at 4°C, then 2½ h at 27°C/65% RH.
