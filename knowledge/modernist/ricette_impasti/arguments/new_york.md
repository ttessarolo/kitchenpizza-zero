## 1. New York Pizza Dough (Master Recipe) <!-- p.45 -->

**Type:** Master Recipe | **Time:** Active 15-20 min / Inactive 48½ h | **Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| **Poolish** | | |
| High-gluten bread flour, 13.5%-15% protein | 60 g | 100 |
| Water | 60 g | 100 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.06 g | 0.1 |
| **Dough** | | |
| Water, 21°C | 350 g | 66.03 |
| Instant dry yeast | 2.8 g | 0.53 |
| High-gluten bread flour, 13.5%-15% protein | 530 g | 100 |
| Diastatic malt powder (optional) | 15 g | 2.83 |
| Fine salt | 14.5 g | 2.74 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 20 g | 3.77 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 590 g (100%), Water 410 g (69.49%), Fat 20 g (3.39%), Salt 14.5 g (2.46%), Diastatic malt powder 15 g (2.54%), Yeast 2.86 g (0.48%)

**Process:** Poolish 12-16 h -> Mix to full gluten development -> Bench rest 20 min -> Preshape ball -> Cold-proof 1-2 d at 4°C

**Flour:** Tony Gemignani California Artisan Type 00 Flour Blend.

## 2. Modernist New York Pizza Dough <!-- p.48 -->

Same base as master plus:
- High-methoxyl pectin: 11.8 g (2.23%) - add with flour
- Dough relaxer options

**Net Contents:** Flour 590 g (100%), Water 420 g (71.19%), Fat 20 g (3.39%), Diastatic malt powder 15 g (2.54%), Salt 14.5 g (2.46%), Pectin 11.8 g (2%), Yeast 2.86 g (0.48%)

### Dough Relaxer Options

| Ingredient | % | Add to |
|---|---|---|
| Papain, powdered | 0.01 | flour |
| Papaya juice, fresh | 0.03 | water |
| Pineapple juice, fresh | 0.01 | water |
| Kiwi juice, fresh | 0.08 | water |
| Adolph's meat tenderizer | 0.01 | flour |

Papain performed best in tests. Pectin increases crust volume.

## 3. Direct New York Pizza Dough <!-- p.49 -->

**Time:** Active 15-20 min / Inactive 6 h

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C | 410 g | 69.49 |
| Instant dry yeast | 2.8 g | 0.47 |
| High-gluten bread flour | 590 g | 100 |
| Diastatic malt powder | 15 g | 2.54 |

**Yield:** ~1 kg. No poolish. Proof 4½-5¼ h at 21°C. Optional cold-proof 1-2 d at 4°C.

## 4. Emergency New York Pizza Dough <!-- p.50 -->

**Time:** Active 10-15 min / Inactive 2½ h

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12% protein | 585 g | 100 |
| Water, 38°C / 100°F | 405 g | 69.23 |
| Extra-virgin olive oil | 20 g | 3.42 |
| Fine salt | 14 g | 2.39 |
| Instant dry yeast | 5.85 g | 1 |

**Yield:** ~1 kg. Mix all at once on medium-high. Bench rest 20 min, proof 2 h at 21°C.
