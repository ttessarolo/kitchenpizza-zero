# Neapolitan Pizza Dough Recipes

<!-- p.38-44 -->

## 1. Neapolitan Pizza Dough (Master Recipe) <!-- p.38 -->

**Type:** Master Recipe | **Time:** Active 15-20 min / Inactive 27 h | **Yield:** ~1 kg

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C / 70°F | 380 g | 62.3 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.24 g | 0.04 |
| Bread flour, 11.5%-12.5% protein, or 00 flour | 610 g | 100 |
| Adolph's meat tenderizer (optional) | 0.06 g | 0.01 |
| Fine salt | 12.15 g | 1.99 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 610 g (100%), Water 380 g (62.3%), Salt 12.15 g (1.99%), Yeast 0.24 g (0.04%)

**Process:** Mix to full gluten development -> Bulk ferment 20-24 h at 21°C -> Divide 250 g -> Ball -> Proof 3 h at 21°C

**Flour:** Le 5 Stagioni Pizza Napoletano 00, Caputo Pizzeria 00, or Polselli Classica 00.

**Key:** Very low yeast (0.04%), long bulk ferment. Final dough temp target: 27°C / 80°F.

## 2. Modernist High-Hydration Neapolitan Pizza Dough <!-- p.40 -->

**Type:** Ingredient Variation | **Time:** Active 20-25 min / Inactive 27½ h

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| **Pregelatinized Flour** | | |
| Water, 21°C | 120 g | 400 |
| 00 flour | 30 g | 100 |
| **Dough** | | |
| Water, 21°C | 380 g | 65.5 |
| Instant dry yeast | 0.24 g | 0.04 |
| 00 flour | 580 g | 100 |
| Meat tenderizer (opt.) | 0.15 g | 0.03 |
| Fine salt | 13 g | 2.24 |

**Net Contents:** Flour 610 g (100%), Water 500 g (81.97%), Salt 13 g (2.13%), Yeast 0.24 g (0.04%)

**Key technique:** Pregelatinize flour by cooking water + flour to 65°C / 175°F, then cool. This enables high hydration (81.97%) while maintaining workability. Target dough temp: 27-28°C / 80-82°F.

## 5. AVPN Neapolitan Pizza Dough <!-- p.43 -->

**Adapted from Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana**

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Water, 21°C | 400 g | 64.52 |
| Fresh yeast | 0.7 g | 0.11 |
| Fine salt | 17 g | 2.74 |
| 00 flour, 12%-13% protein | 620 g | 100 |

**Yield:** ~1 kg. **Flour W:** 280-310.

**Process:** Mix by hand (madia) or machine -> Bulk ferment 12-18 h at 20-20.5°C -> Divide 250 g -> Proof 6-8 h at 20-20.5°C.

**Key:** Uses fresh yeast. Lower temperature (20-20.5°C). Very long double fermentation.

## 6. Emergency Neapolitan Pizza Dough <!-- p.44 -->

| Ingredient | Weight | Baker's % |
|---|---|---|
| Bread/00 flour | 585 g | 100 |
| Water, 38°C / 100°F | 400 g | 68.38 |
| Fine salt | 20 g | 3.42 |
| Instant dry yeast | 5.85 g | 1 |

**Yield:** ~1 kg. **Time:** Active 10-15 min / Inactive 2½ h.

**Key:** Higher water temp (38°C) and high yeast (1%) for fast proofing. Mix all at once on medium-high speed. Bench rest 20 min, proof 2 h at 21°C.
